Recommended Shows To Watch

Hover over gifs to read description and click on title to go to trailer.

The Haunting of Hill House


Flashing between past and present, a fractured family confronts haunting memories of their old home and the terrifying events that drove them from it.

The Witcher


Geralt of Rivia, a mutated monster-hunter for hire, journeys toward his destiny in a turbulent world where people often prove more wicked than beasts.

9-1-1(FOX)


Explores the high-pressure experiences of the first responders who are thrust into the most frightening, shocking and heart-stopping situations.

Chef's Table:BBQ


The Emmy-nominated series shifts its focus to the art of the barbecue, featuring accomplished chefs from the US, Australia and Mexico.

Mindhunter


In the late 1970s two FBI agents expand criminal science by delving into the psychology of murder and getting uneasily close to all-too-real monsters.

Our Planet


Experience our planet's natural beauty and examine how climate change impacts all living creatures in this ambitious documentary of spectacular scope.
